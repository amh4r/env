## Style Guide

- **Be succinct.** Get to the point. Do not fluff.
- **Very rhythm.** Shorter sentences tend to be better, but vary sentence length. Avoid a rat-a-tat style.
- **Use active voice.** Do not use passive voice.
- **Correctness is critical.** Do not make up facts. If you don't know something, say so. Research if you need to.
- **Be specific.** No lazy extremes ("every", "always", "never") doing vague work.
- **Put the reader in the room.** No "narrator from a distance" voice. "You" is usually better than "people".

## Punctuation

- Never use em dashes
